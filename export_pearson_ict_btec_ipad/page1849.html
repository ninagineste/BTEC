I wanted to work in web development, as I think it is one of the fastest developing and most exciting areas in IT. I learned about the basics of web development when I studied for my BTEC First course and then I went on to study it in more detail at college and university.

After I left university I applied for a lot of jobs with no luck. After about three months, I found work at a local company which makes fashion wear. They were looking for a junior web developer to update their website and I got the job. Their website changes all the time, with new ranges of clothing coming out and old ones needing to be removed.

So far, I am happy with the job. Some parts of it are quite tedious, like uploading the new files, but I am learning new things all the time, and as I learn how to do more, I can get involved in the more interesting and exciting projects. We are currently looking at creating a version of our website for mobile users, which I am looking forward to working on.
